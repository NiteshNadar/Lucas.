# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Local businesses and startup companies seeking automation solutions.

## Product Purpose
To market Lucas Automation's services, showcase expertise, and generate leads by demonstrating that automation is accessible and profitable.

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy, affordable, profitable.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, overly complex tech jargon interfaces.

## Design Principles
1. **Show, Don't Tell**: Let the results and value of automation speak for themselves.
2. **Professional yet Accessible**: Build trust without feeling overly expensive or out of reach.
3. **Value-Driven**: Highlight profitability and tangible business benefits.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG AA compliance. Ensure clear contrast and readable typography.
